Admission Process 2026

Eligibility Criteria

1. Undergraduate (B.Tech)

Passed 10+2 with PCM.
• Gen: 45% Agg. | Reserved: 40% Agg.
+ Valid Score in JCECEB / JEE.

2. Diploma (Polytechnic)

Passed SSC (10th) with vocational/ITI qualifications.

How to Apply

  1. Check Eligibility: Ensure you meet criteria.
  2. Registration: Apply via college portal or JCECEB.
  3. Verification: Submit Mark Sheets, TC, Migration, Photos.
  4. Fee Payment: Confirm seat by paying fees.
For queries: admission@kkcemdhanbad.ac.in
